### Scale and workload characteristics

- **Scale (cities)**: 105 cities
- **Scale (URLs per round)**: ~210,000 URLs (≈2,000 per city)
- **Workload (text per round)**: ~3 GB unstructured text
- **Workload (iterations)**: 5 rounds (≈15 GB processed cumulatively)
- **Architecture shift**: crawl-and-store → agent-based on-the-fly (reduced persistent storage of full-text corpora)
