## Collaboration and roles

This repository documents work conducted within WP2 of the EU Horizon Europe
project CULTIVATE (Grant Agreement No. 101083377).

### Institutional collaboration

WP2 is led by Trinity College Dublin (TCD) under the academic leadership of
Professor Anna Davies (Project Coordinator and WP2 Leader).

The development of large-scale automation tools for data collection and
classification was carried out by Dublin City University (DCU) through the
ADAPT Research Centre.

### Scope of this repository

While automation tools were developed and maintained by DCU/ADAPT, this
repository focuses on the downstream data pipeline, including data modelling,
cloud ingestion, validation, and analytical workflows led within TCD WP2.
